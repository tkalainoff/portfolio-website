A Studio of Makers

a process of restless discovery

Leroy Street Studio, 2019

New York, NY

The exhibition, A Studio of Makers, celebrated the work of LSS studio members conceived, designed, and made outside the workplace. At LSS, their design process is anchored by curiosity, invention and craft. They come from a variety of artistic backgrounds and deeply value the act of making. Every piece in A Studio of Makers reflected a process of restless discovery â€” a captivation with the handmade, a drive to create, and a exploration of what inspires them. Although their interests vary, the interrogation of materials, details, and mediums is the common denominator informing what they do both inside and outside the studio.
