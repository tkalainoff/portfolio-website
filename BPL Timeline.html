Brooklyn Public Library - Sunset Park Timeline

celebrating participatory design

Leroy Street Studio, 2019

New York, NY

The focal point of the exhibit Behind the Screens, this timeline captures the participatory design process embedded within a much longer complex timeline of capital projects - we are talking over several years here! The timeline gives context to Brooklyn Public Library Sunset Park's soon to be finished new building and the library that was built for the community in the interim. It serves as a road map and key to the exhibit and highlights collaborators who have contributed along the way.
