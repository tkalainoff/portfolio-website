Pone Up

keep friends accountable, while keeping friendships intact

In collaboration with Christina Weisner + Kurt Texter, 2016

Charlottesville, VA

The app Pone Up (pronounced "pony up") marries the playfulness of Snapchat with the ease and efficiency in financial exchanges of PayPal and Venmo. The goal of the app is to keep friendships intact by providing an independent and unbiased method for tracking agreements and challenges between friends.
