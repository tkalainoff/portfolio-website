Lunar New Year

years of the pig, rat + ox

Leroy Street Studio, 2019-2021

New York, NY

Leroy Street Studio hosts an annual Lunar New Year celebration with their Chinatown neighbors. The event is meant to invite the community into their office space while supporting local businesses through the food and festivities of the evening. As an architecture studio, they value art and design and have a love for modernism - these graphics embody those interests while serving as a party invitation.
