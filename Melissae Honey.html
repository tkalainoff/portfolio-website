Melissae Honey

truth in transparency

Altamar Brands, 2017

Charlottesville, VA

Melissae Honey believes in the truth of transparency. Their honey is from healthy bees and the best stewards that care for them. Melissae's goal is to educate consumers and highlight examples of good forage + good husbandry that deliver delicious flavors, with the hope that by sharing what 'good' truly looks and tastes like, that consumers will demand more from their food and farmers.

Monofloral honey manifests fascinating and distinct flavor profiles. The intent was to present our honey in such a way that highlighted the potential for variety of intrinsic qualities of color, density, flavor pairings and more. The honey is "globally local", cultivated with the devoted small farmer mentality, but from all over the world. It was important to evoke a distinct time and place of each batch, transparent through and through.
