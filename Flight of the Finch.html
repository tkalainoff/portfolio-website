Flight of the Finch

are you happy now?

Dan Sartain, 2015

Charlottesville, VA

After the death of his impulsive girlfriend, a hardened man grapples with memories of their tumultuous relationship in an attempt to come to grips with its end. Flight of the Finch is a student film by Dan Sartain that was produced and submitted to film festivals in October 2015. It needed a crowd-funding campaign to reach ~$3000 in order to supplement grant proposals and get production running. The crowdfunding campaign was successful to complete production and was released in September of 2016.
