about

I'm a Filipino/German multi-disciplinary designer currently living in Brooklyn, NY. I've moved around a bunch and as a Third Culture Kid my background and upbringing greatly influence my design perspective and how I move about the world.

With experience at the intersection of data + design, I've always been interested in equitable access + understanding of information. Information is power, though it has little to no value without context and a good story.

I love to deeply listen to the experiences of individuals, communities and organizations and I live to communicate those experiences through analytic data, thoughtful visuals - print, web, or even physical objects + spaces.

I have a BS in Architecture from the University of Virginia and certificates in Data Visualization and Data Analytics from Parsons School of Design and Columbia Engineering.
