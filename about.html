With experience at the intersection of data + design, I've always been interested in equitable access + understanding of information. Information is power, though it has little to no value to the common person without context and a good story.

I love to deeply listen to the experiences of individuals, communities and organizations and I live to communicate those experiences through analytic data, thoughtful visuals - print, web, or even physical objects + spaces.

I have a BS in Architecture from the University of Virginia and certificates in Data Visualization and Data Analytics from Parsons School of Design and Columbia Engineering.
